PID Controller for Parcel Stabilization

The Federal Aviation Administration heavily regulates drones; as a result, despite economic and customer incentives, wide scale drone delivery adoption is halted. Drones must meet high safety specifications; therefore, stabilization for drone delivery via tether was constructed and tested. The settling time and oscillations were significantly reduced from ~30 s to ~10 s. The solution has the potential to reduce the system's invasiveness, consequently increasing safety.

Estimating Permissible Life Cycle of Turboprop Propellers

Aging turboprop general aircrafts are exeeding their permissible life cycle. Although most aging aircrafts are outfitted with modern engines and avionics, propellers are often overlooked because its failure is difficult to forcast; therefore CFD-FEA models will delineate propeller's Von Mises and modal characteristics, which will used to extrapolate its permissible life cycle.

Without an appropriate experiment strategy, the quantity of experiments are proportional to the square of the quantity of variables. The project attempts to minimize the quantity of experiments by interpolating data from experimental extremas. Model will be compared to finalized experiment.
